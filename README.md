## Friend Finder
Only one item to help you keep track of your friends (or enemies) on the server.

## Usage:

Shift + Right-click on another player to bind the Friend Finder to them.
Right-click (while not targeting a player) to make your bound player glow for a few seconds — visible even through walls — no matter where they are on the server.

## This is my first mod ever. 
I've always wanted an item like this, but I haven't found a single one that applies a glow effect to another player, just compasses or other some special HUD effects that don't really fit in vanilla Minecraft.
